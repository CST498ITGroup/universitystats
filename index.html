About Us: Our main goal is to create a user-interface for a database of the United States university statistics.

Looking at raw data in a database or spreadsheet can be confusing to most users; however, our design will display the data in a user-friendly Graphical User Interface (GUI). One intended user would be the federal and state governments to perform data analytics to possibly determine how government funding is assisting universities by looking at the number of enrolled students, graduation rates, and if the student/faculty ratio is appropriate for the university. We plan on using blockchain to track logins into the application for security purposes. With these security measures, our application will be compliant with federal privacy laws such as the Family Education Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).
